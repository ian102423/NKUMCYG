about

NKUMC Youth Group is located in Brentwood, Tennessee. Currently around 60-70 youth are in this program and it continues to grow. Under the leadership of Pastor Isaac Hwang and the Student leadership team, the youth group program continues to expand and reach the hearts of those in Middle Tennessee. Various fellowship praise go on throughout the year, with the Winter Retreat being a main event to close off the year. Come join the NKUMC youth group on March 17 for our first worship night and experience the power of God!
