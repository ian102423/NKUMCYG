about

NKUMC Youth Group is located in Brentwood, Tennessee. Currently around 60-70 youth are in this program and it continues to grow. Under the leadership of Pastor Isaac Hwang and the Student leadership team, the youth group program continues to expand and reach the hearts of those in Middle Tennessee. Various fellowship praise go on throughout the year, with the Winter Retreat being a main event to close off the year. Come join the NKUMC youth group on March 17 for our first worship night and experience the power of God!

praise

praise team

The new NKUMC YG Praise/Worship team was formed in the summer of 2016. Going under the name "Illuminate" this team has grown into a stellar band in the course of one and a half years and continues to grow in both in experience and spiritually. Consisting of thirteen members, this extremely talented group decided to reach out to the communities in the Middle Tennessee area in order to spread connect the people of God through music. Although they have only been a been for less than two years, with the grace of God they have gotten very far.

worship

friday night

On Friday nights worship starts at 8 O’clock. This time is for fellowship between the youth members. After praise, the rest of the service is devoted into four different events depending on the week. Revival (1st Friday of the month) Game night (2nd Friday of the month ) Prayer night (3rd Friday of the month) Small groups (4th Friday of the month) Come join NKUMC YG’s Friday services because you really experience the love of God!

sunday morning

On Sunday mornings, worship starts at 10 o’clock. Service consists of Worship and sermon for one hour. Then we transition into Bible study from 11 o’clock to 11:45 where you are put into a bible study dependent on grade level. Then you get to eat lunch and spend time with the other members of the YG!

It's All About God's Presence
Worship creates a space for us to experience the tangible presence of our good Father. We believe it's what we're all created for.

Our only and burning passion is to build the Church and the Kingdom of God on earth and see the people of our day-to-day being freed to fulfill their purpose and calling.

We believe in people – we believe in their potential and also in their incredible ability to influence the world with good. Our leadership team has a beautiful history together spanning many years. Our team is diverse and we've created a culture that believes we're all empowered leaders.
