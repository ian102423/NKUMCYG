about

NKUMC Youth Group is located in Brentwood, Tennessee. Currently around 60-70 youth are in this program and it continues to grow. Under the leadership of Pastor Isaac Hwang and the Student leadership team, the youth group program continues to expand and reach the hearts of those in Middle Tennessee. Various fellowship praise go on throughout the year, with the Winter Retreat being a main event to close off the year. Come join the NKUMC youth group on March 17 for our first worship night and experience the power of God!

praise

praise team

The new NKUMC YG Praise/Worship team was formed in the summer of 2016. Going under the name "Illuminate or insert name here" this team has grown into a stellar band in the course of one and a half years and continues to grow in both in experience and spiritually. Consisting of thirteen members, this extremely talented group decided to reach out to the communities in the Middle Tennessee area in order to spread connect the people of God through music. Although they have only been a been for less than two years, with the grace of God they have gotten very far.
